More Information

I teach out of my studio in my home in Mississauga, and I would love it if you helped to fill it with music! If you sign up for lessons with me, payment for the month is due during the first lesson of the month. Lesson cancellations will be refunded if at least 24 hours notice is given, or in case of an emergency (including illness or suspected illness--please do not come if you feel unwell!) I also require masks to be worn while in my home, due to the ongoing nature of COVID-19. I am happy to teach everyone: children, teenagers, or adults looking to learn a new skill for fun! Please don't hesitate to reach out if you have any questions for me.
