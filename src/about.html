About Claire

Thank you for stopping by my website! I am an aspiring front-end developer and freelancer, as well as a violinist and violist. Since graduating with a BMus in Musicology and sound studies with a minor in anthropology from the University of Victoria, I have spent my time learning how to code. I have taken courses from SheCodes (SheCodes Basics, Pro, Responsive, and React), and on Codecademy. I now enjoy creating projects using HTML5, CSS, JavaScript, and React.js, and I'm excited to be a lifelong learner and create beautiful, responsive and polished products. You can see what I've been working on by checking out my Upwork profile here or looking at my portfolio. My background in music has helped me develop many skills that are valuable in the tech industry; creative problem solving, self-discipline, accountability, attention to detail, and teamwork & collaboration (from ensemble work in quartets and orchestras) to name a few. I have been playing the violin since the age of three, and in my music career have played with the Lethbridge Symphony Orchestra, the UVic Orchestra, the Brampton Rose Orchestra, the UVic Collegium baroque ensemble, and have travelled to the Stirling Bridge International Arts Festival with the Regina Chamber Strings and attended the Tafelmusik Baroque Summer Institute.
